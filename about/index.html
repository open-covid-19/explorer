Open COVID-19

Where does the data come from?

The dataset used in the Open COVID-19 map is the Open COVID-19 Dataset, which contains total confirmed positive cases and total fatal cases for all reporting countries as well as subregions of Australia, China, Italy, Spain and USA.

How often is the data updated?

Data is updated daily for most countries and subregions. It's also worth noting that the dates in the data represent when the data is reported to the relevant authorities, like WHO or ECDC, which is normally done the following day.

How is the forecast done?

The forecast is done by fitting the data to a specialized logistic curve, called Gompertz function. For a walk-through of why this model was chosen over other potential alternatives, see this analysis.
